Six Human-Agent Interaction Patterns for Enterprise Agent Systems

Beyond the chat window — how humans and AI agents actually collaborate in real enterprise workflows.

Jeff Zhang · LinkedIn Article →

When AI Agents move from consumer chat interfaces into real enterprise operations, "conversation" becomes just one of six distinct interaction patterns — and often not the most frequent one. Click any card below to explore the interactive prototype.

0

Home — Agent Ops Hub

The central command center where operators monitor all agent activities, review pending tasks, and access the unified workspace.
1

Supervisory — Real-Time Monitoring

Watch agents work in real time. The human sets boundaries and intervenes only on exceptions — like an air traffic controller managing autonomous flights.
🗼 Metaphor: Air Traffic Control Tower
2

Adjudicative — Structured Case Review

Agent presents evidence and reasoning; the human renders the final judgment. Structured, auditable, compliant — the judicial bench of AI operations.
⚖️ Metaphor: Judge Reviewing a Case Brief
3

Calibrative — Feedback & Correction

When the agent gets it wrong, the human doesn't just override — they teach. Every correction becomes training data that improves future performance.
🎓 Metaphor: Mentoring an Intern
4

Strategic — Policy & Configuration

Humans define the rules of the game; agents execute within those boundaries. Adjust signal weights, thresholds, and strategies — then backtest before deploying.
📜 Metaphor: Legislator Drafting Law
5

Exploratory — Conversational Investigation

The classic "chat with AI" — but purpose-built for investigation. Human and agent co-explore open-ended problems through multi-turn dialogue.
🔍 Metaphor: Detective & Partner
6

Retrospective — Performance Review

Periodic review of agent performance at scale — accuracy, cost, drift, and skill health. The performance review cycle that keeps the system trustworthy.
📊 Metaphor: Quarterly Performance Review
